AICAP Certification Guide

AI Collaboration and Provenance Standard for Collaborative Authorship

What is AICAP?

AICAP is a voluntary standard for transparently documenting and validating the use of AI tools in the creation of books, research, and other authored works. It affirms that the author maintained control over the creative process and ethically integrated AI.

Steps to Certify Your Work

  1. Download and apply the AICAP Checklist
  2. Declare AI assistance in your acknowledgments
  3. Publish your audit or provenance process (PDF, GitHub, etc.)
  4. Display the AICAP badge in your book or site
